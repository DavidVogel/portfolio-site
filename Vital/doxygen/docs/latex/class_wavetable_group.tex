\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A class representing a group of Wavetable\+Components combined to form part of a wavetable. 

A \doxylink{class_wavetable_group}{Wavetable\+Group} holds multiple Wavetable\+Components (sources or modifiers) that work together to generate or transform a waveform. By combining and averaging their outputs, the group produces a single resulting waveform for any given position. Wavetable\+Groups can be stacked by the \doxylink{class_wavetable_creator}{Wavetable\+Creator} to form complex, layered sounds.
