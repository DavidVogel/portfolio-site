\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Manages the display and interaction of wavetable keyframes and groups on a timeline. 

\doxylink{class_wavetable_organizer}{Wavetable\+Organizer} provides a scrollable, interactive timeline of wavetable groups and their components\textquotesingle{} keyframes. Users can create, remove, select, and drag keyframes to rearrange positions. The organizer integrates with a \doxylink{class_wavetable_creator}{Wavetable\+Creator} and updates keyframe positions within components.

It also notifies attached listeners about changes in selected frames and frame positions.
