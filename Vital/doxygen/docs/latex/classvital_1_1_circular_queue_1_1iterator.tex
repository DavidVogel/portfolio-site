\subsubsection*{template$<$class T$>$\newline
class vital\+::\+Circular\+Queue$<$ T $>$\+::iterator}
A forward and backward iterator for iterating over the elements in the \doxylink{classvital_1_1_circular_queue}{Circular\+Queue}. 

The iterator supports increment and decrement operations and wraps around the circular buffer.
