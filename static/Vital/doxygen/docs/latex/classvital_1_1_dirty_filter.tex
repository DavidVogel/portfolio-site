\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A nonlinear filter that produces a "{}dirty"{} and saturated sound, ideal for adding character to the signal. 

The \doxylink{classvital_1_1_dirty_filter}{Dirty\+Filter} implements a filter with a nonlinear, saturating signal path. It supports multiple filter styles including 12 d\+B/oct and 24 d\+B/oct slopes, as well as a dual mode for more complex responses (e.\+g., dual notch bands). Resonance, drive, and blend between low, band, and high responses can all be controlled. Internal saturations and nonlinearities produce a gritty, warm sound that can be more aggressive than traditional state-\/variable filters.

Inputs\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item k\+Audio\+: \doxylink{structvital_1_1_input}{Input} audio signal.
\end{DoxyItemize}

\doxylink{structvital_1_1_output}{Output}\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item The processed, dirtied, and filtered audio signal.
\end{DoxyItemize}

The \doxylink{classvital_1_1_dirty_filter}{Dirty\+Filter} uses a series of one-\/pole filters combined with nonlinear saturations. It dynamically adjusts parameters (coefficient, resonance, drive, and blend amounts) based on the provided Filter\+State, and can also respond to MIDI note-\/based cutoff frequencies. Through careful setting of drive and resonance, it can produce strong character and even distortion.
